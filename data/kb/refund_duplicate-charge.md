# What to do if I was charged twice

Duplicate charges are almost always temporary holds from the card network, not actual debits. Please wait up to 5 business days: the duplicate usually disappears from your statement on its own.

If after 5 business days the duplicate is still present, contact support@shophive.example with the order number, the last 4 digits of the card and the date of both charges (a screenshot of the statement is helpful). We verify against our payment processor and, if a true duplicate is confirmed, we refund it immediately. The refund typically appears in your account within 3 to 7 business days.
