# How to speak with a human agent

To talk to a human agent, the fastest channel is live chat from our help centre during operating hours (Mon-Fri 9-18, Sat 10-14). Our chat starts with an automated assistant; type "agent" or "human" at any time and you will be transferred to the first available representative.

Average wait time is under 3 minutes. If you prefer voice, call the regional support number listed in our help centre — the same wait-time applies. For complex issues we recommend email, which routes you to a senior agent within one business day.
