# Step by step to request a refund

To request a refund, open the order from your dashboard and click "Request refund". You will be asked to select a reason (defective product, wrong item, no longer needed, etc.) and to confirm the items to be refunded.

For "no longer needed" returns within our 30-day window, you receive a prepaid return label by email within one business day. Once we receive the returned items in their original condition, the refund is issued to your original payment method within 5 to 10 business days.

For defective products or wrong items, no return is required before the refund: we process it as soon as the issue is confirmed with photos.
