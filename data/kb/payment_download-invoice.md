# How to download my invoice

Invoices are generated automatically for every paid order and are available in PDF format from your order history. Open the order and click "Download invoice" — the PDF includes tax breakdown, billing details and payment method, formatted to comply with the tax requirements of the country of purchase.

If you need to change the billing data on an invoice (company name, tax ID) please do so within 7 days of the order date by opening the order and clicking "Edit billing details"; after that period the invoice is final and we issue a credit note instead. Bulk download of multiple invoices is available for business accounts.
