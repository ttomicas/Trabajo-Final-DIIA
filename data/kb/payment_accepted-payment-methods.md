# Accepted payment methods

ShopHive accepts the following payment methods, available based on your country at checkout:

- **Credit and debit cards**: Visa, Mastercard, American Express.
- **Digital wallets**: Apple Pay, Google Pay, PayPal.
- **Bank transfer**: in selected regions, for orders above 100 USD.
- **Buy Now Pay Later** (BNPL): Klarna and Affirm, where available.
- **Store credit**: balance from previous refunds or gift cards.

All payments are processed by PCI-DSS certified providers and we never store full card details on our servers. If your preferred method is not visible at checkout, it is likely unavailable in your region.
