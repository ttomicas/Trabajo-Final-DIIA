# What to do if my account got locked

If your ShopHive account has been locked, it's usually due to security reasons to protect your information. Don't worry, we're here to help you regain access quickly. The most common reason for a lock is multiple unsuccessful login attempts.

To unlock your account, please follow these steps:
*   Visit our "Forgot Password" page at shophive.example/reset-password.
*   Enter the email address associated with your ShopHive account. You will receive an email with instructions to reset your password.
*   If you do not receive the email within 15 minutes, please check your spam or junk folder.
*   If you continue to experience issues or believe your account was locked in error, please contact our Customer Support team at support@shophive.example or call us at 1-800-SHOP-HIVE during our business hours, Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM EST. They can verify your identity and assist with unlocking your account.
