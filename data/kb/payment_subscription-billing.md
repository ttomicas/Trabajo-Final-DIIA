# How subscription billing works

ShopHive subscriptions (recurring deliveries and digital memberships) are billed automatically at the start of each cycle using the default payment method on file. You receive a billing receipt by email within minutes of the charge.

Key rules:

- You can pause, skip or cancel a subscription any time before the next billing date from "Account" → "Subscriptions".
- A failed charge triggers up to three retry attempts over 7 days; if all fail, the subscription is paused until you update the payment method.
- Cancellations are effective at the end of the current paid period — you keep access until that date and are not charged again.
