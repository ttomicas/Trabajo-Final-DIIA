# Currency conversion and FX charges

Prices on ShopHive are shown in the currency of your shipping country. When you pay with a card in a different currency than your bank account, your bank — not ShopHive — performs the conversion using their exchange rate and may add an international transaction fee, typically between 1% and 3%.

The total in your local currency may therefore differ slightly from the figure shown on the receipt. We do not control or share any portion of these bank fees. To avoid them, you can pay in your local currency where it is offered as an option at checkout, or use a multi-currency wallet such as PayPal.
