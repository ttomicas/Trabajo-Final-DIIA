# Email verification process

To ensure the security of your ShopHive account and to enable all features, we require email verification. Once you create an account or update your email address, you will receive a verification email from us. Please check your inbox, including your spam or junk folders, as it may sometimes be filtered there. This email contains a unique link that you need to click to confirm your email address.

This verification process is crucial for receiving important account updates, order confirmations, and promotional offers. The verification link is valid for 24 hours. If the link expires, you can request a new verification email by visiting your account settings page at shophive.example/account and clicking "Resend Verification Email." If you encounter any issues or do not receive the email within 15 minutes, please contact our customer support team at support@shophive.example. Our team is available Monday to Friday, from 9 AM to 6 PM.
