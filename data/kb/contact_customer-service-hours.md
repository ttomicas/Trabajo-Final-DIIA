# Customer service operating hours

Our customer service operates from Monday to Friday, 9:00 to 18:00 local time, and on Saturdays from 10:00 to 14:00. We are closed on Sundays and on the public holidays of each country we serve.

During operating hours we respond to live chat within 2 minutes on average, and to phone calls within 60 seconds. Outside operating hours, email tickets continue to be received and are answered first thing on the next business day. For urgent matters affecting an active delivery, the carrier's tracking link is the fastest channel even outside our hours.
