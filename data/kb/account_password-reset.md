# How to reset a forgotten password

If you've forgotten your ShopHive password, don't worry! Resetting it is a straightforward process. Simply navigate to the login page and click on the "Forgot Password?" link. You'll be prompted to enter the email address associated with your ShopHive account. Once submitted, we'll send you an email containing a secure link to reset your password. Please check your inbox, including your spam or junk folders, as this email should arrive within a few minutes.

The password reset link is valid for 24 hours for your security. If you don't receive the email or the link expires, you can initiate the process again by following the same steps. If you continue to experience issues accessing your account, our customer support team is available to assist you Monday through Friday, from 9 AM to 6 PM, via email at support@shophive.example.
