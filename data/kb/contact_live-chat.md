# Live chat availability and how to use

Live chat is the fastest way to reach us during operating hours (Mon-Fri 9-18, Sat 10-14). To start, click the chat icon on the bottom-right corner of any page on shophive.example or open it from the help centre.

The conversation begins with an automated assistant that can resolve common questions in seconds (tracking, returns, password reset). If your case needs a person, type "agent" and you are transferred to the queue — average wait is under 3 minutes. A full transcript of every conversation is sent to your registered email when the chat ends.
