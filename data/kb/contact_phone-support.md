# Phone support availability

Phone support is available during our standard operating hours (Mon-Fri 9-18 local time, Sat 10-14). Each country we operate in has a dedicated number listed in our help centre; calls are toll-free from landlines and standard mobile rates apply.

Average answer time is under 60 seconds during business hours. The call menu starts with a brief routing question (orders, refunds, billing, technical) and connects you to the corresponding team. We recommend phone for time-sensitive issues; for non-urgent matters, email or chat are equally effective and leave a written record.
