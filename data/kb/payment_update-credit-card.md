# How to update my credit card on file

To update or replace the credit card on file, go to "Account" → "Payment methods" and click "Add new card". After verifying the new card with a 1 USD pre-authorisation (refunded automatically), you can mark it as the default method for future orders and subscriptions.

To remove the old card, click the three-dot menu on its tile and select "Delete". You cannot delete a card that is currently the only payment method linked to an active subscription; first add a replacement, mark it as default, then delete the old one. Card data is encrypted at rest and never exposed in the dashboard.
