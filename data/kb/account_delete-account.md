# How to delete or close my account

To delete or close your ShopHive account, please follow these steps. You can initiate this process directly through your account settings. Navigate to "My Account," then select "Account Settings." Within this section, you will find an option to "Close Account." Please be aware that closing your account is permanent and will result in the irreversible deletion of all your personal data, order history, and saved preferences.

Before proceeding, ensure you have completed any outstanding orders or returns. If you have any active subscriptions or pending refunds, these will be cancelled and processed accordingly. Refunds will be issued to your original payment method and typically take between 5 to 10 business days to appear. If you encounter any difficulties or have questions, our customer support team is available Monday to Friday, from 9 AM to 6 PM, via email at support@shophive.example.
