# How to track the status of an order

You can track every ShopHive order from your account dashboard under "My Orders". Each entry shows the current stage (received, packed, shipped, in transit, delivered) and the estimated delivery date.

For shipped orders you also get a tracking number that links directly to the carrier's website (UPS, FedEx, DHL or local postal service). The same tracking number is sent by email at the moment the shipment leaves our warehouse.

If the status has not changed for more than 72 business hours, write to support@shophive.example with your order number so we can escalate to the carrier on your behalf.
