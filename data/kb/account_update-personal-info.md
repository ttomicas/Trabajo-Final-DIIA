# How to update personal information

You can easily update your personal information by logging into your ShopHive account. Once logged in, navigate to the "My Account" section, typically found in the top right corner of the homepage. From there, you'll see options to edit your profile details, including your name, email address, phone number, and shipping addresses. Simply click on the relevant section, make your desired changes, and click "Save" to confirm.

If you encounter any difficulties or have questions about updating your information, our customer support team is available to assist you. You can reach us via email at support@shophive.example or by calling us during our business hours, Monday to Friday, from 9 AM to 6 PM. Please note that any changes to your primary email address may require a brief verification process to ensure account security.
