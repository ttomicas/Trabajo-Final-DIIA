# Available shipping options and times

ShopHive offers three shipping tiers, all calculated at checkout based on destination and weight:

- **Standard**: 5 to 8 business days. Free on orders above 50 USD.
- **Express**: 2 to 3 business days. Flat fee depending on country.
- **Same day** (selected metro areas): orders placed before 11:00 local time arrive the same day.

International shipments may take longer due to customs clearance. The estimated delivery window for each method is shown before you confirm the order. Saturday delivery is available in some regions for an additional fee.
