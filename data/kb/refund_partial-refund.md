# When partial refunds apply

Partial refunds are issued in a few specific scenarios:

- Items returned with signs of use, missing accessories or damaged original packaging.
- Multi-item orders where only some items are returned.
- Promotional bundles where breaking the bundle changes the unit price.
- Late returns received after the 30-day window but within 45 days.

In all of these cases, the deduction is communicated by email before the refund is finalised, with a clear breakdown of the original price, the deduction reason and the final amount credited. If you do not agree with a partial refund decision, you can reply to that email to request a review within 7 days.
