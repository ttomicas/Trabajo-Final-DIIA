# Email channels for support

For email support, use the address that matches your topic so your request goes directly to the right team:

- **support@shophive.example** — general support, orders, deliveries.
- **refunds@shophive.example** — refunds, returns and disputes.
- **billing@shophive.example** — invoices, payment failures, taxes.
- **press@shophive.example** — press, media and partnerships.

Please include your order number whenever applicable. Each email is acknowledged within minutes by an automatic ticket-creation reply, and answered by a human agent within one business day during the work week.
