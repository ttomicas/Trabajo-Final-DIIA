# Refund policy and timeframes

Our refund policy is designed to be straightforward and customer-friendly. If you need to return an item, please initiate the process within 30 days of receiving your order. You can start a return by visiting your order history in your ShopHive account or by contacting our customer support team. Once we receive your returned item and confirm its condition, we will process your refund.

Refunds are typically issued to the original payment method. Please allow 5-10 business days for the refund to appear in your account, depending on your bank or card issuer. For any questions or assistance with a return, our support team is available Monday to Friday, from 9 AM to 6 PM, via email at support@shophive.example.
