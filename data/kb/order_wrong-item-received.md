# I received the wrong item — what now

We are sorry for the mix-up. To resolve it quickly, please send a photo of the item received and a photo of the shipping label to support@shophive.example, quoting your order number. We answer within one business day with a prepaid return label.

You do not need to wait for the wrong item to be returned: as soon as we confirm the mismatch from the photos, we ship the correct item via priority shipping at no charge. The wrong item must be returned within 14 days using the prepaid label we provide.
