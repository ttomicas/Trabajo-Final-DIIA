# What to do if my payment was declined

A declined payment can come from three sources: the card issuer, our fraud-check system, or a temporary network error. Common causes:

- Insufficient funds or daily spend limit reached.
- Card not enabled for online or international purchases.
- 3-D Secure verification was not completed.
- Billing address mismatch with the card statement.

Try again after a few minutes, double-checking that the billing address matches what is registered with your bank. If the decline persists, contact your bank to authorise the merchant "ShopHive". Repeated declines may temporarily block our checkout for security; in that case, write to support@shophive.example.
