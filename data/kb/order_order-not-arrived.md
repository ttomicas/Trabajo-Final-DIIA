# What to do if my order didn't arrive

If your tracking shows the order as delivered but you have not received it, please wait 48 business hours: occasionally carriers mark packages early and they appear the next day. Also check with neighbours, building doormen and any safe place the carrier may have used.

If after 48 hours the order is still missing, contact support@shophive.example with your order number and a description of the delivery location. We open an investigation with the carrier within one business day and, if the package is confirmed lost, we ship a replacement at no cost or issue a full refund — whichever you prefer.
