# Where the refund money is credited

Refunds always return to the original payment method used at checkout:

- Credit and debit cards: the amount is credited back to the same card. Typical processing time is 5 to 10 business days, although in some banks it can take up to one full billing cycle to appear.
- Bank transfers: the refund is sent to the bank account used to pay; 2 to 5 business days.
- ShopHive store credit: instant.

If the original card has been cancelled or replaced, contact support@shophive.example: we can issue store credit instead, or, in some cases, transfer to a different account after additional verification.
