# Setting up two-factor authentication

To enhance the security of your ShopHive account, we strongly recommend enabling two-factor authentication (2FA). This adds an extra layer of protection by requiring a second verification step beyond your password when logging in. Setting up 2FA is quick and can be done directly from your account settings.

To get started, navigate to the "Security" section within your ShopHive account. From there, you'll see an option to enable two-factor authentication. You'll be guided through a simple process to link your preferred verification method, such as a code sent via SMS to your registered phone number or using an authenticator app. Once activated, you'll be prompted for this second verification each time you log in from a new device. This process typically takes less than two minutes to complete. If you encounter any issues, our customer support team is available Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM, to assist you.
