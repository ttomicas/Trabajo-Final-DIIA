# How to cancel an order before shipment

Orders can be cancelled at no cost while their status is still "Processing" or "Packed". To cancel, open the order from your dashboard and click "Cancel order"; the refund is issued to your original payment method within 3 to 5 business days.

Once the order moves to "Shipped" it can no longer be cancelled from the website. In that case, you have two options:

- Refuse the package at delivery; it will be returned to us automatically.
- Accept it and start a standard return within 30 days of delivery for a full refund minus the return shipping fee.
