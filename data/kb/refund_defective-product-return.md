# Returning a defective product

If a product arrives damaged or stops working within the warranty period, you do not need to ship it back before getting your money back. Email support@shophive.example with:

- Your order number.
- Two or three photos that clearly show the defect.
- A short description of the problem.

We confirm the issue within one business day and trigger a refund or replacement at your choice. Defective items must be discarded responsibly; we cover the cost of disposal in regions where it applies. The refund is credited within 5 to 10 business days after confirmation.
