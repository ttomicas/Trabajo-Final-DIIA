# Typical response time SLA

Our service level agreement for response times is:

- **Live chat**: first response under 2 minutes during operating hours.
- **Phone**: first response under 60 seconds during operating hours.
- **Email**: first response within 1 business day; resolution within 3 business days for most tickets.
- **Social media**: first response within 4 business hours.

Tickets are answered in the language of the original message whenever it matches one of our supported languages. Complex investigations (lost shipments, payment disputes) may exceed the standard resolution times; in those cases we communicate progress every 48 business hours until closure.
